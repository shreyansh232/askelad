# Runway and Burn Rate Management

## Overview
Track runway milestones, calculate the point of "Default Alive" vs "Default Dead", and design strategies for expense containment.

## When to Use
- Managing cash constraints in low-funding conditions.
- Determining if hiring or expansion is financially feasible.
- Evaluating startup survival timelines.

## Instructions
1. Calculate the growth rate required to reach cash flow break-even before runway expires (Default Alive threshold).
2. Establish key runway alerts (6 months warning, 3 months red alert).
3. If runway is < 6 months, freeze non-essential spending and focus exclusively on revenue-generating actions or active fundraising.
