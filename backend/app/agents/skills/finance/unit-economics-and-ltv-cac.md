# Unit Economics and LTV:CAC Framework

## Overview
Perform audits on startup unit economics to verify that customer acquisition channels are sustainable and profitable.

## When to Use
- Auditing marketing and advertising efficiency.
- Deciding whether to scale customer acquisition spend.
- Checking gross margins.

## Instructions
1. Calculate Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) = Total Sales & Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired.
2. Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) = (ARPU * Gross Margin %) / Churn Rate.
3. Verify LTV:CAC Ratio > 3:1.
4. Keep the CAC Payback Period under 12 months for optimal cash flow efficiency.
