# Startup Financial Modeling

## Overview
Create simple, formula-driven spreadsheets to project revenue, forecast operational expenses, and maintain safe cash management protocols.

## When to Use
- Building financial slides for pitch decks.
- Calculating runway and monthly burn rate.
- Planning hiring budgets or marketing campaign spends.

## Instructions
1. Build a bottom-up revenue forecast based on customer acquisition funnel conversion rates.
2. Itemize monthly expenses (payroll, software tools, marketing/ads, legal/rent).
3. Compute the monthly net burn (Total Expenses - Total Revenue).
4. Project Runway: Cash Balance / Net Burn Rate.
