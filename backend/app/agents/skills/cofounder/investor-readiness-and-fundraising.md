# Investor Readiness and Fundraising

## Overview
Assess startup traction, prepare data rooms, select SAFE note terms, and coordinate check closing momentum.

## When to Use
- Preparing to raise angel, pre-seed, or seed capital.
- Reviewing pitch deck slide structure.
- Setting up valuation caps and discount rates.

## Instructions
1. Verify key traction metrics (MRR, MoM growth, Cohort Retention) match standard benchmarks.
2. Structure the pitch deck (Problem, Solution, Market Size, Traction, Team, The Ask).
3. Use SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) templates for early checks.
4. Build momentum by securing smaller checks early to create urgency.
