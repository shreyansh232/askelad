# Programmatic SEO and GTM (Go-To-Market)

## Overview
Generate templated, data-driven landing pages at scale to capture high-intent long-tail keywords, and coordinate early-stage GTM launch events.

## When to Use
- Building an organic acquisition funnel.
- Planning a launch on Product Hunt, Hacker News, or Twitter.
- Targeting niche customer keywords.

## Instructions
1. Find high-volume, low-difficulty search queries related to user workflows.
2. Build page templates dynamically rendering unique data based on parameters.
3. Establish launch sequences (e.g. outreach templates, social assets, community outreach).
