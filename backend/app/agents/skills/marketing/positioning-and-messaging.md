# Positioning and Messaging

## Overview
Differentiate your startup by defining a clear value proposition, identifying target personas, and crafting messages that resonate with core buyer pain points.

## When to Use
- Designing the home page hero section copy.
- Positioning against a major competitor.
- Writing launch pitches for platforms like Product Hunt.

## Instructions
1. Outline the competitor landscape and highlight your unique differentiator.
2. Define the core user pain point and how the product addresses it.
3. Keep messaging simple: focus on benefits (time saved, revenue generated) rather than purely listing features.
