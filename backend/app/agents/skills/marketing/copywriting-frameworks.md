# Copywriting Frameworks

## Overview
Applies copywriting models to emails, landing pages, and ad copy to maximize click-through and signup rates.

## When to Use
- Writing marketing email sequences.
- Drafting landing page copy.
- Creating copy for paid campaigns.

## Instructions
1. Use **PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)**:
   - Identify the user's primary challenge.
   - Describe why the problem is painful if left unresolved.
   - Present the product as the solution.
2. Use **AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)**:
   - Hook the reader with a strong headline.
   - Spark interest with details.
   - Build desire with benefits.
   - Call to action immediately.
